<h2 class="h3 fw-bold mb-4">PG Soft Table Games: Rules, Limits, and Gameplay</h2>

<h3 class="h5 fw-bold mt-4 mb-3">Dragon Tiger</h3>
<p class="mb-3">Dragon Tiger is a two-card comparison game. The dealer places one card in the Dragon position and one in the Tiger position. You bet on which will be higher, or you can bet on a tie. The game moves fast—each round takes 20–30 seconds. On Robopragma, our Dragon Tiger tables range from conservative minimums (suitable for Medan or Bandung players testing the game) to high-limit tables for experienced players. The payout for a correct Dragon or Tiger pick is 1:1; a tie pays 8:1 or 11:1 depending on the table variant.</p>

<h3 class="h5 fw-bold mt-4 mb-3">Sic Bo</h3>
<p class="mb-3">Sic Bo uses three dice. The dealer shakes them in a cage, and you bet on the outcome—total sum, specific numbers, or combinations. Payouts range from 1:1 (even money on high/low) to 150:1 (for a specific triple). Sic Bo is slower-paced than Dragon Tiger, giving you time to study the odds and place multiple bets per round. Our Sic Bo tables on Robopragma cater to players who enjoy strategic betting and longer session rhythms.</p>

<h3 class="h5 fw-bold mt-4 mb-3">Roulette</h3>
<p class="mb-3">European roulette (37 numbers, 0–36) is our standard. You bet on single numbers, ranges, colours, or odd/even. The dealer spins the wheel, and the ball lands in a pocket. Payouts for a single number are 35:1; red/black or odd/even pay 1:1. Our roulette tables on Robopragma feature clear betting layouts and real-time spin animations so you follow every moment.</p>
